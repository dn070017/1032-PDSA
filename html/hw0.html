Homework assignment:

Frequent words in the essay
Please create a class named WordCount to read a short essay (words are separated by a single space) and print the top three frequent words (case insensitive) to the standard output.
Only words with a length longer than 3 (>3) are considered.
Command:

Input example:hw0_in.txt

it was the best of times it was the worst of times
it was the age of wisdom it was the age of foolishness
it was the epoch of belief it was the epoch of incredulity
it was the season of light it was the season of darkness
it was the spring of hope it was the winter of despair
season darkness winter season darkness winter
season darkness
season

Output example:

season 6
darkness 4
winter 3
